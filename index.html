About Us

Here at the Two East Tabloid, we treasure one thing above all else: reporting accuracy. Our stories are not only completely accurate, but they're also completely correct stories. Our staff includes such journalistic genius as Will "Wiru" Reyes, Tamba "Tambae/Tambino/Mr. Drilla/Mr. October" Monrose, and Justin "Sad Boy" Xiao. We are residents of Two East in Next House at MIT. We hope you enjoy our publications!
